More About Us

CompostOn is a product made by a team of Harvard students to combat food waste.

We're Green

We enable a more sustainable method for restaurants to reduce food waste in restaurants in the United States.

Convenient Pickup

We connect gardeners with restaurants who produce food waste to give gardeners options for free compost.

Scheduling

We make it easy to schedule a waste pickup in advance. Today, tomorrow, next week: CompostOn.

Our Mission

About us

Our mission is to reduce food waste by repurposing it as compost.

Reducing waste

We want to Incentivise companies that use our platform to convince other restaurants around them to participate in sustainability by reducing food waste.

Reduce program

Our hope is to drive down the statistic that 33% of food in the world is wasted. Our platform is a significant step towards getting there through taking simple steps.

recycle more

Our design solution is easily integrated into existing restaurant infrastructures and does not disrupt existing restaurant flows. The design is easy to scale because it is easy to adopted whether you are an independent restaurant, large corporate chain of restaurants, and mom-and-pop restaurant (such as food trucks).
